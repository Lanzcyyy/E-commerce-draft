Our Story

Dosmil was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves to feel comfortable and confident in what they wear. Our founder, Alex, started the brand after a solo backpacking trip across Europe. Feeling the sting of loneliness in new cities, Alex realized that a great shirt could be more than just clothing—it could be a conversation starter, a source of comfort, and a reminder that you’re never truly alone.

Inspired by the people met and stories shared along the way, Alex returned home determined to create shirts that bring people together. Today, Nevelonely stands for connection, comfort, and community—one shirt at a time.
